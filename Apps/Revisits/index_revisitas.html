Your ministry companion!

Return visits is a tool to help you keep track of your Bible studies as well as your time reports.



Features



Dynamic Island

Visualize your service time on the Dynamic Island on compatible iPhones.

Live Activities

Visualize your service time on the lock screen with Live Activities.

Service Facets

Classify your reports and plan your hours for each month, now you can separately report your Bethel service from regular service.

Revisits

Record your revisits along with their contact information and a history of what was discussed on each visit.

Territories

Locate your revisits and studies on the map and get directions from your current location.

Notifications

Schedule interactive notifications to remind you to prepare your revisits and studies or when reaching the time limit.

Reports

Keep track of your service as well as your hour goal, share your report on any messaging app you like.

iCloud

Keep your information synchronized and backed up on all your devices with iCloud.

Agenda

Organize your revisits by territories or dates, swipe to delete or tap for quick actions.

Customization

Your app, your style, choose from different colors, icons, and markers as well as different ways to navigate the app to feel at home.

Widgets

Stay up to date with your monthly progress with report widgets, there are three different sizes and many colors to choose from.

iPad Pointer

Use a mouse or trackpad to navigate the app on iPad.

Universal App

Download the app for free for iPhone, iPad, Watch, visionPro or Mac.

Notifications

Schedule a weekly notification, just enable weekly notification when creating a reminder for a new visit.

Goals

Now you can schedule your service goals with fractions of hours and special service days.
